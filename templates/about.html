Why a Website?

Built for my E115-Intro to Programming final project, this website serves as a central hub to show off my projects. Instead of sending someone to GitHub for them to look through repositories and then giving multitudes of links to my Dropbox-hosted photos - I finally can say "just go to my website". This hub also serves a secondary purpose; while I have a place to store and document my projects, I also get a chance to learn and practice web development. This is a learning experience as much as it is a showcase.
